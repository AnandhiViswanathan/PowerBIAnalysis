# PowerBI - Sales Analysis

📊 Total Gross Sales by Country and Product (Stacked Bar Chart)

Description:
This visual shows the total gross sales across different countries, with each bar segmented by product categories.

Insight Definition:
The stacked bar chart helps users compare sales performance between countries while also understanding which products contribute most to the revenue within each country.

Business Value:

Identifies top-performing countries

Shows product contribution to sales within each region

Helps guide regional product strategies

🌍 Total Gross Sales by Country (Pie Chart – % Split)

Description:
This pie chart represents the percentage share of total gross sales contributed by each country.

Insight Definition:
It provides a market share view of revenue distribution, making it easy to see which countries contribute the most to overall sales.

Business Value:

Highlights key markets driving revenue

Shows relative contribution of each country

Helps identify regions with growth potential

📈 Total Profit by Segment with Year-on-Year Comparison

Description:
This visualization compares total profit generated by different business or customer segments across multiple years.

Insight Definition:
The year-on-year comparison allows stakeholders to analyze profit trends, growth, or decline within each segment over time.

Business Value:

Tracks segment profitability trends

Identifies high-growth or declining segments

Supports strategic investment decisions

📦 Total Units Sold by Products Over Time

Description:
This chart shows the number of units sold for each product across a time period such as months or years.

Insight Definition:
It highlights product demand trends and sales patterns over time, allowing businesses to monitor growth and seasonality.

Business Value:

Identifies high-demand products

Reveals seasonal sales patterns

Supports inventory and supply planning
